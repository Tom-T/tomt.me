About

I’m a senior Linux, DevOps, and platform engineer with 25+ years working exclusively in Linux — RHEL, CentOS, and Ubuntu — across the full lifecycle from bare metal to CI/CD. For roughly four and a half years I was the sole administrator and on-call responder for the Linux production servers behind a banking SaaS serving financial-institution customers: the kind of environment where uptime is not negotiable and a quiet pager is the whole job.

What sets my output apart today is leverage. I build and operate real systems by orchestrating AI agents — treating them as a team I direct — which lets me move from idea to shipped software at a pace that used to require a whole squad. Underneath it all is the same craft: automation, infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Kubernetes), and clean Bash and Python.
